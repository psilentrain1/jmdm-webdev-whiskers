About Us

Whiskers & Wags Pet Grooming was born from a simple belief: pets deserve a grooming experience that feels like a spa day, not a stressful appointment. Founded by Sarah Martinez, whose lifelong passion for animals evolved into over 15 years of professional grooming expertise, our salon creates a calm, loving environment for your furry family members.

Sarah's philosophy is simple: listen to the animals, respect their boundaries, and make them feel safe. This approach has made Whiskers & Wags the trusted choice for pet parents who want the very best for their companions.
